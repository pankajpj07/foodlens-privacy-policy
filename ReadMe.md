Ever checked your Swiggy or Zomato app for your total 
lifetime spending? You can't — because they don't show it.

FoodLens does.

In 60 seconds, FoodLens analyzes your complete order 
history and shows you the real numbers — total spent 
all time, this month, this year, and exactly where 
that money went.

WHAT YOU'LL SEE

💰 Total spent — all time, this year, this month
📊 Monthly spending chart — see your worst months
🏆 Top restaurants ranked by how much you've given them
🍽️ Most ordered dishes
🥗 Veg vs Non-Veg spending breakdown
📅 Filter by any custom date range
🛒 Swiggy Instamart orders included

WHY IT EXISTS

Food delivery apps make money when you order more. 
Showing you how much you've spent would make you 
think twice — so they never built it.

FoodLens is that feature they chose not to build.

HOW TO USE IT

1. Open Swiggy or Zomato in Chrome
2. Make sure you're logged in
3. Click the FoodLens icon in your toolbar
4. Hit Analyze — takes about 60 seconds
5. See everything

Works with Swiggy food, Swiggy Instamart, and Zomato.

YOUR DATA STAYS WITH YOU

FoodLens runs entirely in your browser. Your order 
history never leaves your device, never touches a 
server, and is never shared with anyone. Not even us.

No account needed. No sign up. Free forever.
